How to Book Water Slide Rentals in Orlando?


If you’re looking for a fun way to cool off this summer, consider booking a waterslide rental in Orlando! Water slides are perfect for any type of event, from birthday parties to corporate events. Here’s how to book one:

  1. Start by browsing the selection of water slides on offer. There are a variety of different types to choose from, including speed slides, body slides, and kiddie pools.
  2. Once you’ve selected the perfect slide for your event, contact the rental company to reserve it. Be sure to provide the date, time, and location of your event.
  3. The rental company will ask for a deposit to hold the slide for your event. This deposit is typically 50% of the total cost of the rental.
  4. Decide what type of water slide you want. There are three main types: Inflatable, Slip & Slide, and the Giant Inflatable Water Slide.
  5. Water slides provide hours of fun for people of all ages. They’re especially fun for birthday parties and school events. The water slide rental company will provide you with everything you need to use the slide, including all the required safety equipment.
